Wellness Challenges

Deep Breathing Exercise

Encourage users to take a break and practice deep breathing for 5 minutes.

Inhale deeply through the nose for a count of 4, hold for 4, and exhale slowly through the mouth for a count of 6.

Mindful Walking

Suggest users take a short mindful walk outdoors or indoors,

focusing on the sensations of each step and the environment around them.

Aim for a 10-minute walk, paying attention to the sights, sounds, and feelings.

Gratitude Journaling

Prompt users to spend a few minutes writing down three things they are grateful for each day.

This practice can help shift focus from stressors to positive aspects of life.

Stretch Breaks

Encourage users to take mini stretch breaks throughout the day to release tension and improve circulation.

Offer simple stretching exercises for the neck, shoulders, arms, and legs that can be done at a desk or in a chair.

Digital Detox

Challenge users to take a break from screens for a designated period, such as one hour before bedtime or during meal times

Encourage them to engage in offline activities like reading, crafting, or spending time with loved ones.

Progressive Muscle Relaxation

Guide users through a progressive muscle relaxation exercise, where they systematically tense and relax different muscle groups in the body.

This can help alleviate physical tension and promote relaxation.

Nature Connection

Encourage users to spend time in nature, whether it's taking a walk in a park, sitting by a tree, or simply gazing at the sky.

Being in nature has been shown to reduce stress and improve mood.

Visualization Exercise

Lead users through a guided visualization exercise where they imagine themselves in a peaceful and calming environment, such as a beach or forest.

Encourage them to engage all their senses in the visualization for maximum relaxation.

Social Connection

Encourage users to reach out to a friend or family member for social support and connection.

Even a brief conversation or message exchange can help alleviate stress and boost mood.

Self-Compassion Practice

Encourage users to practice self-compassion by offering themselves kindness and understanding during times of stress.

Prompt them to repeat self-affirming statements or engage in activities that bring them joy and comfort.
